About Fun Work
I tell stories through pixels.

While studying English & Media Studies at UC Berkeley, I stumbled upon the mysterious world of design. I was fascinated by how user experience design combined my passions of storytelling, representation, and thoughtful analysis into one discipline. Since then, I've been designing!


I'm currently a UI/UX designer at Fulldive, a mobile virtual reality startup. Previously, I've been a design consultant at Berkeley Innovation, where I worked on projects for various startups. I also conducted design research with BEST Lab. Beyond UI/UX, I've been a program manager at UC Berkeley's on-campus computer facilities, ETS, working to improve the campus job experience through design.


On my free time, I like hanging out in museums, chilling in parks, and cutting things with lasers.
